About Me

Award Winning

Pianist

Nuno Marques has developed an extensive, international performing career from his current base in New York City.

A Doctor of Musical Arts, Dr. Marques is a versatile musician, equally comfortable playing solo, chamber music, teaching, or collaborating with other forms of artistic expression.

Holds a Master’s Degree in Piano Performance from the Royal College of Music, London; and a second Master’s Degree in Piano Pedagogy from the Aveiro University in Portugal.

Having first moved to New York to attend NYU— the Steinhardt School, under the tutelage of José Ramón Méndez— he was awarded the prestigious Reinhold Scholarship and taught classes at the NYU piano department.

Director of an International Piano Festival

Porto Pianofest in Porto, Portugal

Nuno Marques is the founder and director of Porto Pianofest, an international piano festival in the beautiful northern city of Porto, Portugal.

Founded in 2016, the festival is now on its 5th successful edition, attracting musicians from over 20 countries and 4 continents.

Nuno himself performs frequently at the festival and serves on the artist faculty. Porto Pianfest takes place every August in Porto, and presents an annual New York City event at Yamaha Artists Services in Manhattan every Spring.

Porto Pianofest presents the highest caliber of artists in concerts throughout the city. Its mission is also to provide performance opportunities and world-class instruction to young pianists that come to advance their careers and artistry.

Performance

As a soloist, Nuno Marques has performed in some of the most prestigious halls, including Carnegie Hall, St. Martin-in-the-Fields, Barbican Hall, and Casa da Musica, amongst many others. He has also performed solo, chamber and with orchestra concerts throughout the world— including in Europe, America, and Asia, where he has twice toured China.

A Doctor of Musical Arts, Dr. Marques is a versatile musician, equally comfortable playing solo, chamber music, teaching, or collaborating with other forms of artistic expression.

Holds a Master’s Degree in Piano Performance from the Royal College of Music, London; and a second Master’s Degree in Piano Pedagogy from the Aveiro University in Portugal.

Having first moved to New York to attend NYU— the Steinhardt School, under the tutelage of José Ramón Méndez— he was awarded the prestigious Reinhold Scholarship and taught classes at the NYU piano department.

Educator

Dr. Marques is an experienced piano pedagogue, presenter and speaker, having taught all levels of piano from beginner to professional.

He serves in the artist faculty at Porto Pianofest, working along world class pedagogues from Northwestern University (Chicago), Escuela Superior Reina Sofia (Madrid), and Université de Montreal (Canada); teaching students from some of the most prestigious Conservatories and Universities from around the world.

Nuno Marques has taught at NYU (New York, USA), privately in London (UK), at the Conservatory of Porto (Portugal), and currently maintains a private studio in Manhattan (New York). Additionally, Dr. Marques is faculty at Bloomingdale School of Music (Manhattan, NY) where he directs the Bloomingdale Piano Training Program, aimed at the most advanced students who wish to study at the pre-professional level.
